# E-Commerce Sales & Customer Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes e-commerce sales, customer behavior, product performance, and payment trends using Power BI.

The dashboard helps stakeholders monitor revenue, customer distribution, order patterns, freight costs, and top-performing product categories.

---

## Tools Used

- Power BI
- SQL
- Excel / CSV
- DAX
- Data Modeling

---

## Key KPIs

- Total Revenue
- Total Orders
- Total Customers
- Average Order Value
- Total Freight Cost
- Revenue Per Customer
- Orders Per Customer

---

## Dashboard Pages

### 1. Executive Summary

Provides a high-level overview of business performance including:

- Revenue Trend
- Revenue by Payment Type
- Top Product Categories by Revenue
- Year-wise Analysis

---

### 2. Customer Analytics

Analyzes customer behavior and distribution:

- Customers by State
- Top Cities by Customers
- Customer Growth Trend
- Customer Distribution Map

---

### 3. Sales Performance

Focuses on product and freight analysis:

- Top Product Categories by Revenue
- Top Product Categories by Freight Cost
- Revenue Trend by Month
- Payment Type Analysis

---

## Business Insights

- Identified top-performing product categories.
- Analyzed customer concentration across states and cities.
- Measured revenue contribution by payment method.
- Evaluated freight cost impact on sales performance.
